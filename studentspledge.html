STUDENT'S PLEDGE



As a student of Sri Vignesh Vidyalaya, I promise that

  1. I will be punctual.

  2. I will do my home work regularly.

  3. I will always converse in English

  4. I will come in complete uniform.

  5. I will respect my parents and teachers

  6. I will be attentive in the class.

  7. I will behave in such a manner, that my teachers and parents would feel proud.

  8. I will finish my work in time.

  9. I will be responsible enough to do my duties.

  10. I will avoid viewing TV for long hours and using mobile phones or any electronic gadgets unnecessarily and for long hours.

  11. I will not absent myself to school for simple reasons.

  12. I will not talk much.

  13. I will not scribble on the walls, furniture or any part of the School.
