SPECIAL FEATURES

FOUNDATION COURSES

School offers Foundation Course From class VI to VIII by T.I.M.E Foundation Course for IIT, JEE & NEET from class IX to XI is offered by FIITJEE Ltd.

SMART ROOMS

Classrooms are modified into smart rooms that add special feature to our school.

COMPUTER LABS

Two well equipped computer labs with nearly 100 systems connected by LAN.

LIBRARY

Library is well equipped with Books, Magazines and Journals for all students and staff for reference and for the pleasure of reading.

SWIMMING POOL

A state of art swimming pool with excellent coach. Apart from the regular swimming classes, students are trained to compete in the state level Competitions also.

SPORTS AND GAMES

Students are involved in various physical activities and tactical challenges. Sports and games activities are organized time to time in our school as a competition for participants of various events like Kho-Kho, Volley Ball, Hand Ball, Throw ball etc. The School generally organize Intra & Inter School Sports Competition for Indoor and Outdoor Games in the Indoor stadium and the huge ground. Students with fervour for Sports enjoy playing many Games and have made it a bright career too

SPOKEN LANGUAGE

Special training is given to students on English Speaking skills with the help of MIKIDS a programme by SIP Academy on Phonics. Equal importance to curricular and co-curricular activities. The School has a beautiful Auditorium with wonderful acoustics and customizable seating arrangement
